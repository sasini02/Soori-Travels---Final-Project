SOLO ROADS OF SRI LANKA

Travelling alone doesn’t have to mean you are alone! Here’s your chance to immerse yourself into a tropical island adventure and make new memories and a pack of new friends for life! Thirsty for a new adventure? Got a passport and adventurous spirit? Here’s your calling this summer – Sri Lanka it is! This limited-edition itinerary explores a side to Sri Lanka that only a few adventure seeking souls will see. Build a closer connection with nature and like-minded travellers from across the globe whom you will call friends at the end of your 12 days exploration of Sri Lanka. Journey through the scenic mountains, quintessential villages, local temples, pine forests, gravel roads and dusty terrains as you experience the authentic Sri Lankan hospitality. The real adventure does not only lie in the places you visit but also in the charming hotels, local cuisine and spirits with the heart pounding Sri Lankan music to add the much needed colour along the way.
