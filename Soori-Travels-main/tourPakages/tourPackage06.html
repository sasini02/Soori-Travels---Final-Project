FAMILY FRIENDLY SRI LANKA

Ever considered taking an Island vacation this summer? A family friendly island awaits your discovery. We are here to guide you every step of the way if Sri Lanka is your choice. Journey through the rich cultural heritage as you experience friendly encounters with the exotic wildlife, dip your toes or your whole being in the alluring emerald waters in the east coast, pause time to go chasing waterfalls in the rain-forests or experience how slow time can get up in the hills as you enjoy the picture-perfect sceneries of the misty mountains, and the rolling tea fields. A one-of-a-kind family friendly itinerary which will give you the perfect blend of adventure, R&R and cultural immersion whilst taking you on an epicurean delight of Sri Lanka’s finest cuisine. Fix your own dates and should you wish to add in a bit of your own flavour into what we have curated – we’d love to add in that spice!

ITINERARY

Overnight stay at Jetwing Blue

DAY 01

Ayubowan! We welcome you and your family to Sri Lanka. On arrival you will meet your chauffeur guide at airport, and drive to your first hotel in Negombo. Late afternoon set out to explore the fishing lifestyle and immerse yourself into the local way of life around Negombo. Hop on board a local boat as you are guided through the scenic lagoons by a local who will bring to life the rich history and natural habitat.

Explore the Negombo Lagoon on a Boat

Overnight stay at Cinnamon Lodge

DAY 02

After a relaxing breakfast be on your way to Habarana. Embrace the change in atmosphere as you enter the culturally rich triangle of Sri Lanka. In the afternoon enjoy a safari ride at the Mineriya National Park, famously known for the great elephant gathering. Share a memorable moment in the wild with the gentle giants of asia roaming free in their natural habitat.

Mineriya Safari

Overnight stay at Cinnamon Lodge

DAY 03

After breakfast, visit the second capital of the Sinhalese kingdom - Polonaruwa. Walk through the ancient city dotted in ruins while you are guided through its mysterious tales which brings to life the richness of the Sri Lankan heritage.

Pollonaruwa day tour

Overnight stay at Cinnamon Lodge

DAY 04

Wake up early this morning to visit the world wonder, a UNESCO world heritage site – Sigiriya. Explore the beauty as well as the ancient engineering tactics used in the past which is bound to marvel you. Afterwards, visit a local abode to enjoy a scrumptious meal. Experience the simple lifestyle of a Sri Lankan while you visit their village and join them as they prepare the most flavourful traditional meal.

Climb Sigiriya
Village trek

Overnight stay at Trinco Blu by Cinnamon

DAY 05

Today you will leave behind the cultural hub and make your way towards the turquoise waters of Trincomalee. Reach the coastal haven in the east as you are introduced to the chrystle clear waters and white sandy beaches which makes time stand still. After lunch enjoy a visit to the culturally rich city guided by your chauffeur guide who will bring to life the stories of the past to make it one worth the visit.

Exploring city wonders of Trinco

Overnight Stay at Trinco Blu by Cinnamon

DAY 06

Make an early start to the day as you head out to the Indian ocean in a sailing catamaran in search of the blue whales. Spend some quality time at sea and don’t forget to brin back to life your childlike spirit. After an eventful day at sea return to your hotel and rest and relax the rest of the day gazing into the horizon during a picture-perfect sunset.

Whale watching sailing tour

Overnight Stay at Trinco Blu by Cinnamon

DAY 07

After a relax breakfast lazy around and hop on a to reach Pigeon Islands to trade a moment with the life under the sea. Relax at the Nilaweli beach afterwards.

Visit Pigeon Island and realx at Nilaweli beach
Realx at Nilaweli beach

Overnight stay at Cinnamon citadel

DAY 08

Today, you will make your way to the last kingdom of Sri Lanka. A city rich in history and cultural heritage. Enroute you will visit a working tea factory to experience the process of leaf to cup. Finally enjoy a finely brewed cuppa Ceylon tea to taste the world’s finest.

Spicy garden in Matale
Geragama tea factory

Overnight stay at Cinnamon citadel

DAY 09

Monring hours will be spent at the city exploring its unique architecture and the lifestyle of the locals. In the evening you will make your way to Gampola, a town closeby to visit the iconic Ambuluwawa tower.

Kandy cultural city
Ambuluwawa tower

Overnight stay at Cinnamon Grand

DAY 10

After a relaxing breakfast in the central capital of the country, you will leave towards the commercial hub of Sri Lanka. In the evening you have the option to enjoy your free time gracing the many shopping malls, relaxing at the hotel by the pool or exploring the city’s finest Art Museums.

Shopping Mall visit
Enjoy a Movie

DAY 11

Day at leisure

Colombo City tour

After an interesting and fun filled holiday in the tropics, it’s time to say goodbye. Until we meet again, have a safe flight back home. Ayubowan!
